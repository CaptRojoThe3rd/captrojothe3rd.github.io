The VRAM on the NES stores the nametables, which are how backgrounds are stored. The NES has two kilobytes of VRAM, storing two nametables.

A nametable has two sections: the tiles and the attributes. The tiles are a 32x30 area (960 bytes) that define, well, what tiles are chosen from the CHR-ROM or CHR-RAM. The attributes are a 64 byte section after the tiles that determine which of 4 color palettes the tiles will choose from.
