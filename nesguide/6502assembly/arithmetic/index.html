Add (ADC) instructions add a value to the accumulator. Subtract (SBC) instructions subtract a value from the accumulator. Bitwise AND (AND) instructions AND the accumulator with a value. Bitwise OR (ORA) instructions OR the accumulator with a value. Bitwise XOR (EOR) instructions XOR the accumulator with a value. Increment/decrement (INC/DEC) increment or decrement the value at a memory address. Similar instructions exist for the X and Y registers (INX/INY). Bit shift instructions (ASL/LSR) shift bits left or right. The bit shifted out is stored in the carry flag, and the bit shifted in is zero. Bit rotate instructions (ROL/ROR) rotate bits left or right. The bit shifted in is copied from the carry flag, and the bit shifted out is copied into the carry flag after the carry flag is shifted in.
